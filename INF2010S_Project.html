Introduction

Computer Security, also known as cyber security or IT security, invloves safeguarding
computing resources,ensuring data integrity, limiting access to authorised users, and
maintaining data confidentiality.

Key Areas of Concern of Computer Security

  • Confidentiality: Ensuresnsures that only computer resources and assests are only
    accessed by authorised partites.
  • Integrity: Computer assest can only be modified by authorised parties or only in
    an authorised manner.
  • Availabilty: Computer Assets are accessible to authorised users at appropriate times
